e-Books

Project TeMe [(T)amil (e)-books for (M)odern (e)-readers]

Around 2000, group of Tamil software engineers mostly residing in abroad initiate a plan called Project Madurai. The main aim of the plan is to digitalize the available nationlaized tamil books (in PDF and/or HTML) and distirbute them through internet for free of cost. Over a decade many titles were converted and used by so many people. But the problem with this content is that it is not adoptable for modern e-readers like Kindle and smart phones. So, in 2017, I have launched a project called TeMe to convert some of the important books to EPUB or MOBI format to adopt these readers. Currently more than 50 titles are available in this link.
Update: (2020) I am about to add books frequently and this time from Tamil Virtual University and Archive. The books are distributed under the same licesense as that of the original digital document from the respective sites.
